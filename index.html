📜 A Brief History of Kentucky Fried Chicken

The story of KFC begins in the 1930s when Colonel Harland Sanders started serving home-cooked meals at a small service station in Corbin, Kentucky. His secret blend of 11 herbs and spices made his fried chicken stand out, laying the foundation for a worldwide favorite.

By the 1950s, KFC became one of the first major fast-food franchises in the United States. Customers loved the taste, consistency, and the value offered through affordable kfc prices, which helped the brand expand rapidly across the country and eventually worldwide.

Today, KFC operates in over 150 countries, including a large presence in the UK, where people enjoy everything from crispy fried chicken to family bundles. The modern menu continues to evolve with seasonal specials, vegan options, and new drinks — but the Colonel's Original Recipe remains at its heart.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions — KFC Menu & Prices UK

Direct answers to the most common questions about the KFC UK menu in 2026.

  • What is the KFC 10 pieces bucket price?

    The 10-piece Bargain Bucket costs £20.99 and contains 10 pieces of Original Recipe chicken without additional sides or drinks. If you want a complete meal for the family, the 10-piece Family Feast at £24.99 includes sides and drinks alongside the chicken.

  • Does KFC offer any meal deals for one person?

    Yes. The Buckets for One range from £6.99 to £11.49 and include chicken pieces, a side, and a drink. Box meals at £11.49 to £12.99 bundle a burger or wrap with fries and a drink for complete single-serving satisfaction.

  • Are KFC prices the same at all UK locations?

    While core menu prices remain largely consistent across the UK, some variation exists between locations due to regional costs and franchise agreements. Airport and motorway service locations typically charge premium prices. Checking your local restaurant's menu through the official app or website confirms exact pricing for your area.

  • What is the cheapest meal at KFC UK?

    The Regular Popcorn Chicken Meal at £6.99 represents one of the most affordable complete meals, including popcorn chicken, a side, and a drink. For the absolute lowest price point, Mini Fillet Burgers and Mini Wraps start at £2.29 each, though they don't include sides or drinks at that price.

  • Does KFC still offer the £5 fill-up or similar budget boxes?

    While the specific "£5 Fill Up" branding varies, KFC's Classic Savers menu fills this role with items like the Mini Fillet Burger Meal at £5.49 and Mini Wrap Meals at £5.49, delivering complete meals for around five pounds.

  • How many people does a KFC party bucket feed?

    The Party Bucket at £39.99 contains enough chicken to feed approximately 8 to 10 people when paired with sides, or 6 to 8 people as the primary food source. The exact number depends on appetite levels and whether you're serving additional items.

  • What vegan options does KFC offer besides the vegan burger?

    Beyond the Vegan Burger at £6.99, plant-based customers can enjoy Signature Fries, Corn Cob, Side Salad, Beans, and select dips including Supercharger, BBQ, and Real Tomato. Drinks include all Pepsi MAX varieties, 7Up Free, Tango Sugar Free, and Oatly-based iced coffees.

  • Are KFC family meals cheaper than ordering individually?

    Yes, family meals and sharing buckets provide better per-item value than individual purchases. A 10-piece Family Feast at £24.99 costs significantly less than buying 10 individual chicken pieces at £2.39 each (which would total £23.90 for chicken alone, before any sides or drinks).

  • What seasonal items is KFC offering right now?

    As of 2026, festive options including Stuffing Tower Burgers, Stuffing Stacker Burgers, and various Festive Double and Triple Buckets appear from November through January. Limited collaborations like the Stranger Things Burger rotate in periodically. Check the official KFC UK website for current seasonal availability.

  • How much is a KFC Zinger Burger Meal?

    A KFC Zinger Burger Meal costs £8.99 in the UK. The Zinger Burger alone is £6.49–£6.99. The Zinger Stacker Meal is around £8.99–£10.99, and the Zinger Supercharger Tower Burger Meal is £9.99.

  • Is KFC halal in the UK?

    Most KFC chicken in the UK is halal, but it varies by branch. Always check with your local store or look for the halal certification displayed in-restaurant. Vegetarian and vegan options are available for non-meat eaters.

  • Can I customize my KFC order online?

    Yes, you can add or remove sides, drinks, and sauces when ordering online via the KFC website or app. The app allows customization, scheduling orders ahead, and earning loyalty points.

  • Does KFC have an ice cream menu?

    KFC UK does not have a traditional ice cream menu. Desserts include cookies and fruity treats. For a cold creamy treat, consider pairing your meal with a Caramel or Chocolate Krunch Shake from the drinks menu.

  • Are KFC chips/fries vegetarian in the UK?

    Yes, KFC chips are vegetarian. However, they may be cooked in shared fryers, so cross-contamination is possible. Always check with staff if you follow strict vegetarian rules.

  • How often does KFC UK update their menu and prices?

    KFC updates its menu and prices every few months or for seasonal specials. Limited-time offers like seasonal burgers and festive bucket bundles rotate regularly, while core menu items remain stable.

  • What time does KFC open in the UK?

    KFC usually opens at 10:00 AM, but times vary by branch. Many locations open earlier in busy urban areas or airport terminals. Check your local restaurant's hours through the official app or website.

  • Can I order KFC for delivery in the UK?

    Yes, you can order online via the KFC website, the KFC App, Uber Eats, Deliveroo, or Just Eat. Delivery services may charge extra depending on distance and platform. Click and collect is available without delivery fees.

  • What are the most popular items on the KFC menu UK?

    Popular items include Zinger Burgers, Fillet Burgers, Original Recipe Chicken, Hot Wings, the 10-piece Bargain Bucket, Family Feasts, and the Caramel Krunch Shake. New seasonal specials like Stuffing Tower Burgers also generate significant interest during winter months.
